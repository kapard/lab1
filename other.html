Lake Kopa

Kopa is a lake on the Shagalaly River near the city of Kokshetau in the Akmola region of Kazakhstan. The surface area of the lake is 14 km2, the catchment area is 3860 km2. The depth of the lake is from 2 to 3 meters. The height of the water mirror is 223.8 meters above sea level. Kopa Lake with adjacent hills is a unique natural environment that gives a bright individual appearance to the city of Kokshetau.
